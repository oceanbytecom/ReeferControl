Inside Smart Guide Pro

How Smart Guide Simplifies Your Repairs

Instead of static text, Smart Guide Pro acts as an interactive technical assistant that adapts to your measurements, automates math lookups, and logs actions for you.

⚡ Interactive Decision Logic

The app guides you through live hardware checks step-by-step. It asks explicit questions about your diagnostics and provides rapid choice buttons based on your multimeter readouts to determine the exact fault path.

📊 Automated Table Lookups

No more manual interpolation or searching through resistance graphs in paper manuals. Enter the current reefer temperature and your Measured Ohm value—the app cross-references factory sensor tables instantly to verify if the component is defective.

🔗 Multi-Alarm Smart Jumps

Reefer units often throw secondary fault codes triggered by a single broken component. Smart Guide analyzes active arrays (e.g., resolving `AL 100` and automatically jumping to `AL 102`), saving you from repetitive diagnostic loops.

📝 Auto-Generated Watch Reports

Every single step you take—voltage limits inside/outside ranges, component resistance records, and cable checks—is automatically compiled into a clear, professional summary under `Actions performed` to save time on your end-of-watch paperwork.
